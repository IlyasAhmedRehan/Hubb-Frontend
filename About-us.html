About us

Over more than 20 years, HUBB has become a
collection of traders and investors with a broad
variety of interests and expertise.

Our new platform aims to provide a place for this community to come together and interact to generate and share investment ideas in a manner that adds value for all our members.

As a company, HUBB has been supporting both traders and investors with analytical tools, information, and education since 1998. At that time, the internet was

revolutionising investment and, for the first time ever, providing individuals with the opportunity to trade online for themselves.

HUBB was founded with a mission to provide decision support tools and education for these pioneers, and while the technology has evolved, the mission remains the same today.
